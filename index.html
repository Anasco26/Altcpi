Who We are

African Land Transport Crime Prevention Initiative (ALTCPI) is a Non-Governmental organization focused on road security and safety of passengers in land transport. We sensitize passengers, investors and operators in land transportation sector on how to use government policy to protect themselves, and provide means through which public can give feedback on government performance and facilitate identification of land transport security challenges, its location and facilitate means of solving them using funding advocacy
